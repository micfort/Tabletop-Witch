ABOUT

Ever had find yourself in a place where your players found a way to destroy everything you had prepared for the session. This is where Tabletop Witch is usefull. Every one can post his or her monsters, items and other stuff on the site, and it is all searchable right through with your phone/tablet. There is even an encounter builder. The phone/tablet can download monster information and keep record of all sorts of information, like HP, conditions and other stuff. Now we only support Pathfinder RPG, but we are working to support D&D 4E and Shadowrun.
